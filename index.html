DeAnna R. Upshaw is a Leadership Development Strategist, AI Consultant, Executive Coach, Certified International Corporate Facilitator, and Certified DISC Consultant who helps organizations empower leaders to execute the vision and achieve measurable results.

With more than 10 years in leadership development and over 25 years in information technology, she bridges the gap between human leadership and artificial intelligence — so leaders don't just adapt to the future, they shape it.

DeAnna integrates these principles into AI-enabled strategies that strengthen leaders, unify teams, and elevate organizational performance from the inside out.
